# SiteBuilder AI - Current Project Documentation

SiteBuilder AI is an advanced, client-side business website generator that empowers users to create professional, branded websites in seconds. 

---

## 🚀 **Key Features**

### 1. **Industry-Specific Templates**
- **Presets**: Carefully curated content for Bakeries, Car Lots, Estate Management, Tech Stores, Groceries, Pharmacies, Hospitals, and Supermarkets.
- **Custom Mode**: An 'Other' option with a dedicated input for users to define any unique industry.

### 2. **Advanced Branding & Customization**
- **30-Color Palette**: A premium grid (3x10) of professional accent colors.
- **Dynamic Preview**: Real-time updates as you type, showing exactly how the final site will look.
- **Inline Editing**: Click directly on any service card or the About Us section in the preview to refine the text.

### 3. **Comprehensive Social & Contact Support**
- **Social Manager**: Dropdown-based system to manage links for Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, and Telegram.
- **Direct Contacts**: Dedicated fields for Phone Number, Business Address, and Contact Email.
- **Smart Footer**: Dynamically renders only the icons and contact info that have been provided.

### 4. **Logical Site Architecture**
- **Navigation Flow**: Clean, sticky menu with effortless smooth-scrolling to:
  1. **Home** (Hero branding)
  2. **Services / Features** (Core business offerings)
  3. **About Us** (Company story/description)
  4. **Contact** (Direct info and socials)

### 5. **Robust State Management**
- **Auto-Save**: All configurations (color choice, social links, contact info, business text) are automatically persisted in `localStorage`.
- **Session Recovery**: Load from exactly where you left off upon page refresh.

---

## 🛠️ **Technical Infrastructure**
- **Frontend**: Pure HTML, Vanilla CSS, and modern JavaScript.
- **Icons**: FontAwesome 6 for professional, scalable vector icons.
- **Typography**: Premium Google Fonts (Outfit, Inter) for a sleek, high-end feel.
- **Persistence**: `localStorage` (key: `premiumSiteState`) for effortless data recovery.
